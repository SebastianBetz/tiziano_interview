Wherever we're going, we're all responsible.

All over South Tyrol, up to 100,000 passengers use public means of transport every day. A number of rules ensure that all transport services are safe and run smoothly. It is therefore vital that everyone observes those rules: Because every single one of us is responsible for our own safety and that of others. A good rule of thumb is to be cautious, considerate and kind with each other.
